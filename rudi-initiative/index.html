Let us tell you a story about when lions rulled Africa’s Savvannah

RUDI is an initiative under the WildNow Foundation Kenya, centred on bridging the gap between science and
Indigenous Knowledge. Through filming, we aim to tell animation stories on diverse indigenous groups in Africa,
their culture and their contribution to our biodiversity. Our stories are centred on inspiring the global mass,
including kids and youth in the African community, to conserve the environment and harness their culture for the
betterment of the continent and its biodiversity.

Rudi's Mission

Rudi’s Mission Our mission is to preserve and educate on African indigenous wildlife
knowledge through storytelling to improve the continent's biodiversity for
better ecological life support.
