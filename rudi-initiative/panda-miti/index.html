Pandamiti is swahili for "Plant trees"

We’ve compiled a list of effective ways to reduce your
environmental footprint. The biggest game changers are
labelled "top tips".

Join the cause

The initiative aims to build a tree planting
culture amongst communities and schools
in different parts of Kenya. We have so far
planted 1 million trees with the support of
the Kenyan government, NGOs, corporates
and public donations.

Pandamiti aims to :

  • Develop school gardening for
    environmental stewardship amongst
    students and guarantee of fresh foods
    and agricultural learning.
  • Educate and learn from communities
    through art and storytelling.
