Get to know us

― OUR IDENTITY

Who we are

WildNow is an African conservation organization working to sustain the
natural world for the benefit of wildlife and it’s people.
We are focused on restoring natural habitats, stopping the mass
extinction of wildlife, and empowering communities through sustainable
ventures.

Daily Undertakings

― CHANGING LIVES AND ECOSTYSTEMS

Our Mission

Our goal is to improve the co-dependency of our biodiversity by reducing
the rate of human-wildlife conflict, educating and advocating for
sustainable urbanization and development.

To achieve this, the organisation is committed to:

  • Improving wildlife habitats with the coexistence of communities
    around the areas.
  • Building Awareness on environmental issues and innovative solutions.
  • Changing the ideology of economic development in Africa with the
    incorporation of wildlife conservation.

Our Objectives
  • Mitigation of climate change by promoting tree planting.
  • Increase public awareness and appreciation of the technical

  • Enhance the technical capabilities of professionals and communities
    in the areas of restoration.
